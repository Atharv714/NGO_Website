Our Story

Working in India since 1996, protecting sight and fighting for disability rights. We work in 13 states, extending eye health services to the least served areas. Enabling people to lead lives of independence and dignity.

Our Impact

Gayatri's Story

Under our School Eye Health Programme, Gayatri was identified with bilateral congenital cataract and was referred to Sightsavers partner hospital for the surgery. Today, Gayatri can see everything clearly and she has also become quite active in school activities.

Shyam Sundar Devi's story

70-year-old Shyam Sundar Devi from Patna had blurry vision in both eyes. After her screening by our health worker she was referred to SSLN Hospital where she was found to have cataract in both eyes and was operated for it

Soni's story

Soni is eight years old who resides in Jehanabad district of Bihar. Soni is blind since birth and studies in a government school who is full of enthusiasm toward life.

Soni's story

19-year-old Sarita is visually impaired. In 2014, Sarita was given accessible devices under Sightsavers Inclusive Education Programme. She later participated in a Self- Defence Instructor Training organised by Sightsavers which transformed her life.
